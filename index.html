Top 5 Levers for Success (Controllable)

  1. Sales Team Enablement for SaaS & Aerial Intelligence: Aggressively train enterprise sales teams to sell the full "Safety Reimagined" portfolio, with a strong emphasis on AI-driven SaaS, including BRINC drones and SkySafe airspace management solutions.
  2. Channel Partner Incentives for SaaS Growth: Create compelling new ARR-based incentive programs for our key partners to drive cloud, subscription, and aerial intelligence sales.
  3. Integrated Product Bundles with Aerial Solutions: Develop easy-to-sell, high-value bundles (e.g., "Rapid Response Drone Kit" or "Airspace Security Package") that combine hardware, AI-driven software, and services, including BRINC and SkySafe.
  4. Targeted Marketing Campaigns Highlighting Aerial Capabilities: Launch vertical-specific marketing campaigns that speak directly to the pain points of our top opportunity areas (Airports, Venues, Prisons), showcasing the unique value of BRINC and SkySafe.
  5. Streamline Deployment & Onboarding for Advanced Solutions: Simplify the customer experience for cloud products and aerial intelligence solutions to accelerate time-to-value and increase customer satisfaction, ensuring seamless integration into existing workflows.

Primary Obstacles to Success

  • Regulatory & Public Perception Challenges for Drones: Navigating complex FAA regulations, local ordinances, and public privacy concerns related to drone operations and aerial surveillance.
  • Integration Complexities with Legacy Systems: Ensuring seamless integration of drone data, counter-drone systems, and AI-driven SaaS into existing, often outdated, public safety and enterprise infrastructure.
  • Customer Inertia & Budgetary Shifts: Convincing customers to move from a capital expenditure (CapEx) model for hardware to an operating expense (OpEx) subscription model for SaaS and drone-as-a-service, requiring a shift in mindset and procurement processes.
  • Competition from Niche Specialists & Large Tech: Facing strong competition from specialized drone manufacturers, counter-drone companies, and broader SaaS vendors in security.
  • AI & Aerial Literacy within Customer Organizations: Addressing the need for training and expertise among public safety personnel to effectively operate, manage, and utilize advanced AI and drone technologies like BRINC and SkySafe.
